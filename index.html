The Social Welfare Information Management System (MSWDO-SWIMS) is a mobile and web application developed for the Municipality Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) in Guiguinto, Bulacan. It improves the management and efficiency of social welfare services by ensuring accurate, up-to-date, and accessible case information.

Before SWIMS, MSWDO handled processes manually, including complex casework involving court referrals and counseling schedules. Paper-based workflows caused delays, inefficiencies, and difficulties in reporting. SWIMS digitized intake forms, case tracking, and reporting ‚Äì streamlining operations and improving service delivery for cases such as children in conflict with the law.
